Blocked Domains

Format Requirements:

  • One domain per line
  • No http://, https://, or protocols
  • No query in URLs
  • Avoid wildcards
  • Duplicate domains will be removed
  • No space or irrelevant characters

Blocked Keywords

Format Requirements:

  • One keyword or phrase per line
  • Case-sensitive or case-insensitive
  • Only _ and - are allowed
  • Maximum 50 characters per keyword
  • Duplicate keywords will be removed
  • No space or irrelevant characters
